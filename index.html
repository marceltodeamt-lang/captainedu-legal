Privacy Policy — Captain Edu

Last updated: 9 May 2026

Short version: Captain Edu is a children-friendly airplane game. We do not collect personal information, do not show ads, and do not use tracking. The only data the game stores is your local game progress (saved on your device) and any in-app purchases you choose to make through your app store account.

2. What data we collect

2.1 Data stored on your device only

The following information is saved locally on your device using browser/app local storage. It never leaves your device and we (the developer) cannot access it:

  • Your game progress (which levels you have unlocked, stars earned)
  • Your virtual coin balance
  • Your owned power-ups (Freeze, Slow Down, Invincible, 2nd Chance)
  • Your selected language and audio preferences

2.2 In-app purchases

If you purchase coins inside the game, the purchase is processed by the app store (Apple App Store or Google Play). We use the third-party service RevenueCat to manage purchase receipts. The data exchanged is limited to:

  • Anonymous purchase identifiers (transaction ID, product ID, purchase date)
  • Receipt validation token (provided by Apple / Google)

We do not see, store or have access to your name, email address, billing address, payment method or credit card details. All payment information is handled exclusively by Apple or Google according to their own privacy policies.

2.3 Data we do NOT collect

  • No personal name, email or phone number
  • No location data (GPS, Wi-Fi, IP-based)
  • No camera, microphone, contacts or photos
  • No advertising identifiers, no tracking cookies, no analytics SDKs
  • No social media integrations

3. Children's privacy (COPPA / GDPR-K)

Captain Edu is designed to be safe for children. In compliance with the United States Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and the European Union General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), Article 8:

  • We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 (US) or under 16 (EU).
  • The game contains no third-party advertising and no behavioural tracking.
  • Before any real-money in-app purchase, the game shows a parental gate (a math problem) that a young child is unlikely to solve, so a parent must consent before purchase.
  • All in-app purchase transactions are also gated by the device's standard app-store password / biometric protection.

If you believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us at support@captainedu.com and we will delete any such information from our records.

4. Third-party services

Captain Edu uses the following third-party services strictly to provide the game's core functionality:

  • Apple App Store / Google Play Billing — to process in-app purchases. Their privacy policies apply: apple.com/legal/privacy / policies.google.com/privacy.
  • RevenueCat — to validate and reconcile purchase receipts. Privacy policy: revenuecat.com/privacy.

These services may, as part of their normal operation, process technical data (device model, OS version, anonymous purchase IDs) — please consult their privacy policies for full details.

5. Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the following rights:

  • Access — request a copy of the data we hold about you (in our case: none beyond anonymous purchase IDs).
  • Erasure — request deletion of any data linked to you.
  • Object / restrict processing — withdraw consent to data processing.
  • Lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

To exercise any of these rights, write to support@captainedu.com.

5.1 Deleting your local data

You can delete all locally-stored game data at any time by uninstalling the app or, on the device, clearing the app's storage / cache from your system settings.

6. Data security

Because Captain Edu does not transmit personal data to our servers, there is no server-side database that could be breached. Local game progress is stored in your device's standard secure storage area and is protected by your device's own security measures.

7. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page shows when it was last changed. Continued use of the game after an update constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
